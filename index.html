Projects

Point of sales

Role: ROR Developer

POS is a system to perform transactions and manage inventories for a store in real-time. It helps its customers by recommending new items in various categories according to previous choices. The system works in both modes, i.e., online and offline. It synchronises with the server through public and private APIs to send all data upwards to make them available online and for further analysis. It also generates data at regular intervals according to the sales on the server to maintain the system, customers, operators, stores, and inventory history. The server stores and manages all these data by USN (Unique Store Number).

Social Media

Role: ROR and UI Developer

This project has many user roles with different actions. It allows users to share their thoughts and content in various forms like posts, images, podcasts, e-books and videos. It allows non-contributors to follow and connect with contributors', authors', speakers' and influencers' accounts to get updates and notifications. It works on a subscription-based model where each contributor can set the subscription fee according to their choice. Contributors have the privilege to organise and promoting online live events via video conferencing and sell their entry tickets on the portal itself.

E-Medical

Role: ROR Developer

E-medical is a system where doctors and patients register themselves. Patients can look for doctors as per their needs and book an online appointment on available slots. Doctors and patients can connect via video conferencing for online consultations. Doctors can send online prescriptions with all the necessary information. In case of any requirements, patients can avail of the clinical visit and consult with the doctor in person. Patients can either take a subscription or pay for each consultation separately. This system keeps the medical history of each patient's consultations with all the doctors.
