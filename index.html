Projects

Point of sales

Role: ROR Developer

The POS system is designed to facilitate real-time transactions and inventory management for stores. It assists customers by recommending new items across various categories based on their previous selections. The system operates in both online and offline modes, synchronizing with the server through both public and private APIs to transmit all data upwards for online availability and further analysis. Additionally, it generates data at regular intervals based on sales on the server to maintain records of the system, customers, operators, stores, and inventory history. All this data is stored and managed by USN (Unique Store Number) on the server.

Social Media

Role: ROR and UI Developer

This project encompasses multiple user roles with distinct actions. It enables users to share their thoughts and content in various forms such as posts, images, podcasts, e-books, and videos. Non-contributors can follow and connect with the accounts of contributors, authors, speakers, and influencers to receive updates and notifications. Operating on a subscription-based model, each contributor can set the subscription fee according to their preference. Contributors have the privilege to organize and promote online live events via video conferencing and sell entry tickets directly on the portal.

E-Medical

Role: ROR Developer

E-medical is a system where doctors and patients register themselves. Patients can search for doctors according to their needs and book appointments online based on available slots. Doctors and patients can connect via video conferencing for online consultations. Doctors can provide online prescriptions with all necessary information. In the event of any requirements, patients can opt for a clinical visit and consult with the doctor in person. Patients can either subscribe or pay for each consultation separately. This system maintains the medical history of each patient's consultations with all doctors.
